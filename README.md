## Research and Publication

Co-author of the accepted APMC 2026 paper:

> Trinh, V., **Nguyen, C.-D.**, Do, M.-N., Le, Q.-H., & Nguyen, T.-T. (2026).  
> *From Synthetic Clinical Scenarios to Reasoning Evaluation: A Small-to-Large Language Model Training Framework.*  
> The 27th Asia Pacific Management Conference (APMC 2026).

The study explores a pediatric virtual-patient framework that combines retrieval-augmented generation, fine-tuned small language models, and large language models for scenario generation and clinical-reasoning evaluation.
